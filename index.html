 King of Hearts / 2019
King of Hearts is a parody of reality dating shows featuring a vegan, an up and coming rapper, and a vaping nudist competing for the love of a lady who does not want to be there. King of Hearts was entered into the 2019 Tampa 48hr competition where a short film has to be produced in 48 hours, and it was nominated for best ensemble.
Cy played "Cy" who is a vaping nudist that is only in it for the money. Cy was nominated for best supporting character.
